Fikir Ethiopia National Association on Intellectual Disabilities

  • Vision
  • Mission
  • Goal
FENAID aspires to see a society fairer and inclusive in all aspects of development for person with intellectual disability.
FENAID strive to improve their socio-economic status of person with intellectual disability by actively involving their parents in collaboration with stakeholders.
The overall goal of the organization is to contribute to the national effort in creating to improve the social economic situation of persons with intellectual disability by empowering with education and making them self-supporting.

What We Do?

We believe that we can save more lifes with you

Education

Fostering inclusive learning environments and dispelling misconceptions about intellectual disabilities.

Poverty Alleviation

Providing economic opportunities and support for families affected by intellectual disabilities.

Advocacy

Empowering individuals to advocate for their rights and promoting societal inclusion.

Economic Empowerment

Offering vocational training and job opportunities for sustainable livelihoods.

Human Rights

Ensuring the rights and dignity of persons with intellectual disabilities are upheld.

Community Engagement

Organizing awareness campaigns and workshops to promote understanding and acceptance.
